Services

We are a group of computer Geeks that decide to join the forces in creating the world-first Open Infrastructure project combining our expertise with the power of Open Source technologies.

We are here to enable you for the Continous Integration/Continous Delivery (CI/CD) philosophy.

Whatever you are an individual, startup, small or medium enterprise or a big corporation you are definitively at the right place.

Analysis

Together we will analyze your needs and possibilities in opening your projects for CI/CD process. At the end of this phase, you will be delivered a complete roadmap to deploy CI/CD into your organization.

Deployment

We will deploy CI/CD into your organization or we will help you to create one. Using our Open Source tools and framework we will automate almost the whole Software Development cycle of your projects. We will train you and will teach you how to fully take advantage of CI/CD deployment.

Maintenance

We will not leave you alone after the Deployment. We will continue to support you and your team in your daily struggle keeping your environment healthy and up to date with the constant changes.

Open Source Projects

We are not only utilizing best of Open Source tools, frameworks, components and services but we are making Research and Development of our own. We do not do yet an operating system but we have a set of API's, plugins and building and release management solutions crafted to suit many needs of modern organizations, potentially scaling them globally.
